# We Published Our Internal Work Policy. Here's Why.

Most agencies keep their internal policies hidden. We just published ours on our website.

Not because it's a marketing play. Because transparency is how we build trust, and trust is the only reason clients come back.

---

## What's in it

Our Work Policy covers everything a team member (or a prospective one) needs to know about working at CiroStack:

- **How we communicate.** Async-first. Daily standups in Slack. Response time expectations for every channel.
- **What "done" means.** Code reviewed. Tests passing. Deployed to staging. Client notified. No shortcuts.
- **Security rules.** No secrets in code. 2FA everywhere. Client data treated as sacred.
- **Professional conduct.** Feedback on work, not people. No ego. The best idea wins.
- **What gets you fired.** Sharing client data. Fabricating work. Harassment. We don't do warnings for these.

---

## Why publish it?

Three reasons:

**1. Clients deserve to know how we operate.**

When you hire CiroStack, you're trusting us with your product. You should know what standards we hold ourselves to. Not a vague "we value quality" statement. The actual rules.

**2. Candidates deserve to know what they're signing up for.**

We're remote-first. We don't track hours. We do expect ownership, clear communication, and senior-level output. If that sounds like your kind of environment, great. If not, we've saved each other a bad hire.

**3. It keeps us accountable.**

A policy that lives in a Google Doc nobody reads is just decoration. A policy on our website is a commitment. If we fall short, you can point at it.

---

## The short version

CiroStack operates on three principles:

1. **Ownership over attendance.** Nobody tracks your hours. Everybody sees your output.
2. **Ship, don't sit.** If you're blocked, say so. Waiting silently is the worst thing you can do.
3. **Fixed scope, fixed price.** Our model depends on efficient execution. Wasted time is wasted margin.

We overlap 10 AM to 2 PM WAT for synchronous work. Outside that window, work when you're productive. We care about results, not presence.

---

## What this means for clients

When you work with us, you're working with a team that has clear standards, written down and followed. That means:

- Your project gets weekly updates, minimum. Even when the update is "on track."
- You'll never be surprised by a missed deadline. If something shifts, you hear it from us first.
- Your code is reviewed, tested, and deployed properly. Every time.
- Your data is handled with the same care we'd want for our own.

---

## What this means for candidates

We're hiring. And we want you to know exactly what you're walking into.

No fake Glassdoor ratings. No "we're like a family" nonsense. We're a team of professionals who do great work, communicate clearly, and respect each other's time.

If that sounds right, check the open roles on our site. If it doesn't, no hard feelings.
